Know your options

AIESEC

This is student organization that help you make world the better place. You can choose from may projects all around the world and not all of them are voluntary some of them give you opportunity to earn some money.

ERASMUS

A very popular exchange program for students to try studying abroad, meet some new people and expand their horizons, while still being able to study. There are some limitations about who can travel, but you still have a chance!

WorkAway

Want to travel but still earn some money, that program might be just for you! Find place that suits you and look for a job you are good at and start your adventure.

Couch Surfing

If you brave enough to live with person you met online for a few weeks then this is opportunity for you. Chceck people who offers room at places you would like to visit and give it a try.
